# Workshops

You can explore a collection of notebooks that have been used in workshops during the FuseTS session. These notebooks serve as comprehensive guides, taking you through various steps and processes covered in the workshops. Additionally, you will find output samples to compare your results and validate your progress. Furthermore, there is Docker files provided to facilitate the seamless setup of your working environment.

Link to the materials: [https://github.com/Open-EO/FuseTS/tree/main/workshops](https://github.com/Open-EO/FuseTS/tree/main/workshops)
